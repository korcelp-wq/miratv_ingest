# Meridian Group
## Strategic Infrastructure Alignment Platform

---

## 1. Purpose

Meridian Group exists to align, architect, and steward long-horizon infrastructure systems across technology, media, AI governance, and physical development.

We do not build isolated products.

We design and align systems that operate together.

---

## 2. Core Positioning

*Meridian = Alignment*
A meridian is a reference line — a point of orientation.

*Group = Structure*
A flexible holding architecture capable of owning and operating multiple ventures.

Meridian Group is:
- Cross-border
- Infrastructure-focused
- Governance-aware
- Long-horizon oriented
- Institutionally credible

---

## 3. Strategic Function

Meridian Group operates as:

- Ecosystem architect
- Capital-aligned infrastructure platform
- Governance-layer integrator
- Systems alignment authority
